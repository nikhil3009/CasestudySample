Original Requirement:

Date of Birth: To ensure content appropriateness based on age.
Gender Identification: Optional field for users who wish to provide this information.
Favorite Genre: A field with the placeholder "Favorite Genre (to personalize your experience)" for users to specify their content preferences.

Misinterpretation:
The collection of Date of Birth, Gender Identification, and even Favorite Genre could be misinterpreted as directly necessary for personalization, where, according to the law, data collection should be limited to what is "adequate, relevant, and reasonably necessary" for the purposes disclosed to the consumer.

Revised Requirement:
To align with the law, it's essential to clarify the purpose of each data collection point more explicitly and ensure it is indeed necessary:

Date of Birth: Clearly state the necessity of this for age-appropriate content recommendations. Consider if this can be achieved through less intrusive means (e.g., simply asking if the user is above a certain age threshold).
Gender Identification: Evaluate the necessity of this information for the service provided. If it is not essential for the personalization process, consider removing this field to adhere to the principle of data minimization.
Favorite Genre: This appears directly relevant to the service of personalizing content recommendations and likely does not need adjustment, provided the use is clearly communicated and consented to by the user.

Omitted Requirement
Original Omission:
The initial requirements document lacks a provision for a privacy notice that includes:

The categories of personal data processed by the controller.
The purpose for processing personal data.
How consumers may exercise their consumer rights, including how a consumer may appeal a controller's decision regarding their request.
The categories of personal data that the controller shares with third parties, if any.
The categories of third parties, if any, with whom the controller shares personal data.
Necessary Change:
To comply with the law and rectify this omission, the platform must:

Implement a Privacy Notice: Develop and prominently display a privacy notice accessible from both the signup and signin pages. This notice should detail the types of data collected, the purposes of data processing, user rights regarding their data, and any third-party data sharing practices.
User Rights Mechanism: Integrate functionalities that allow users to access, review, and manage their personal data directly from their account settings. This includes options for data correction, deletion, and the ability to opt-out of certain data processing or sharing practices.
Transparency on Data Sharing: Clearly disclose if any user data is shared with third parties and for what purposes, ensuring users have the option to consent to or opt-out of such arrangements.
